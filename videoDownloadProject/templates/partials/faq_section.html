Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about downloads, formats, speed, and safety.

1. Which format should I choose first? +

If you need speed, pick a lower resolution. If you need visual quality, choose the highest available video format your plan allows. For music or podcasts, audio-only options are usually the most efficient.

2. Why are some formats disabled in the list? +

Disabled items are formats outside your current access policy. The platform filters options by plan level and media availability, so you only see downloadable choices for your account tier.

3. Can I download multiple videos from one URL? +

Yes. Playlist links can generate multiple jobs. Each job is processed in queue order and tracked in the progress panel, so you can monitor status without re-submitting each entry manually.

4. What if a download fails during processing? +

Failed jobs are marked in history with their status, and you can retry from the same workflow. Most failures are temporary network/provider issues or unavailable source formats.
